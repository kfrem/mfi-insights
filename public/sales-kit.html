Built for Full Bank of Ghana
Compliance. From Day One.

MFI Clarity is purpose-built for Ghana's microfinance regulatory framework — ensuring every licensed institution meets its obligations under the Banks and Specialised Deposit-Taking Institutions Act, 2016 (Act 930), the Microfinance and Money Lenders Act, and the Bank of Ghana's Microfinance Licensing Guidelines. MFI Clarity enables supervisory efficiency through standardised, accurate, real-time data.

Core Compliance Capabilities
📊

BOG Classification of Advances

Automatic loan classification into Current, OLEM, Substandard, Doubtful, and Loss buckets per BOG guidelines. Provision rates applied automatically: Current 1%, OLEM 5%, Substandard 25%, Doubtful 50%, Loss 100%. Provision totals updated in real-time as loan performance changes.

🏦

Capital Adequacy Ratio (CAR) Calculator

Real-time CAR computation with risk-weighted assets breakdown. Core capital and supplementary capital components tracked separately. Tier-specific minimum ratios monitored continuously with visual compliance status indicators. Alerts when CAR approaches regulatory minimums.

📋

Prudential Returns

Digital preparation of all required prudential return forms per BOG's reporting schedule. Submission status tracking and deadline monitoring. All returns linked to the tamper-resistant audit trail. Period-end data locked to prevent post-submission alterations.

🔒

AML/CFT Compliance

Full AML reports aligned with the Anti-Money Laundering, Anti-Terrorism and Proliferation Financing Act (AML/CFT&P), 2022. Suspicious transaction flagging with narrative fields. Complete Customer Due Diligence records. STR (Suspicious Transaction Report) history maintained for regulatory inspection.

🪪

KYC / Customer Due Diligence

Ghana Card number validation (format: GHA-XXXXXXXXX-X) enforced at data entry. Risk categorisation (LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH) assigned to every client. Source of funds documented. Proof of residence type recorded. Group/cooperative member KYC individually captured. Expiry date tracking for identity documents.

💧

Liquidity Ratio Monitoring

Real-time Liquidity Coverage Ratio (LCR) and Net Stable Funding Ratio (NSFR) tracking. BOG minimum thresholds enforced per tier classification. Deposit stability metrics tracked. Liquidity status displayed prominently on regulatory dashboard with colour-coded compliance badges.

🔐

Tamper-Resistant Audit Trail

Every system action — loan creation, modification, approval, repayment posting, user login — is logged with user ID, timestamp, and action type. Audit records cannot be edited or deleted. Fully searchable and filterable for examiner use. Supports both onsite and offsite supervisory examinations.

🏗️

Multi-Tier Licensing Compliance

Tier 1 Rural Banks, Tier 2 Savings & Loans, Tier 3 Finance Houses, Tier 4 MFIs, and MFC (Micro Finance Companies) each have their specific loan limits, CAR thresholds, and liquidity requirements enforced within the system. Tier configuration is admin-controlled with change audit logging.

Data Security Architecture
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) enforced on all data tables — no cross-tenant data access possible
  • Multi-tenant architecture with strict organisation isolation
  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) — 5 distinct permission levels
  • Supabase PostgreSQL with enterprise-grade AES-256 encryption at rest
  • TLS 1.3 encryption in transit for all API communications
  • Automatic session expiry and re-authentication enforcement
  • Password reset via verified email channel only
