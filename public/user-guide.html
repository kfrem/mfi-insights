Getting Started

Your First 10 Minutes with MFI Clarity

Follow these steps to go from zero to fully operational. The entire setup takes less than 10 minutes — no IT expertise required.

1
Register Your Account
Visit mfi-clarity.lovable.app and click "Staff Login" → "Register". Enter your email and a strong password. You'll receive a verification email — click the link to activate your account.
💡 Tip

Use your official work email address. This email will be linked to your role and all audit trail entries.

2
Create Your Organisation
After email verification, you'll be directed to the Organisation Onboarding screen. Enter your institution's legal name, trading name, registration number, BoG license number, physical address, and contact details. Select your BoG MFI Tier (Tier 1-4).
💡 Why Tier Selection Matters

Your tier determines capital requirements, lending limits, single obligor limits, and reporting frequency. The system enforces these automatically.

3
Invite Your Team
Navigate to User Management from the sidebar. Click "Add User" and enter each team member's email. Assign them a role: Admin, Manager, Field Officer, Teller, or Board Director. They'll receive an invitation email with login instructions.
4
Start Adding Clients
Go to Data Entry → "New Client". Fill in client details including Ghana Card number, date of birth, occupation, source of funds, and risk category. The system supports Individual, Group, Cooperative, and SME client types.
5
Create Your First Loan
Go to Data Entry → "New Loan Application". Search for a client, select loan type, enter principal amount, interest rate, term, and repayment frequency. The system will calculate the amortisation schedule and check affordability before submission.

Client Management

Registering & Managing Clients

MFI Clarity supports four client types: Individual, Group, Cooperative, and SME. Each type captures the specific KYC information required by BoG.

Registering a New Individual Client

1
Navigate to Data Entry → New Client
Select "Individual" as the client type. The form will adapt to show the required fields for individual KYC.
2
Enter Personal Details
Required fields: First name, last name, date of birth, gender, nationality, Ghana Card number, Ghana Card expiry date, occupation, source of funds, and risk category (Low/Medium/High).
3
Enter Financial Details
Monthly income and monthly expenses are used for loan affordability calculations. Enter accurate figures — the system will automatically assess debt-to-income ratios.
4
Upload Documents
After creation, go to the client's profile and upload supporting documents: Ghana Card copy, proof of residence, business registration (for SMEs), and any other KYC documentation.

Group Clients

For group lending, first register the group as a "Group" client type with a group name. Then add individual members using the Group Members modal. Each member has their own KYC details, and you can assign roles (Leader, Secretary, Member). The group's collective financials are calculated automatically.

Client Search & Assignment

Use the client search feature across the platform to find clients by name, ID, or phone number. Managers can assign clients to specific Field Officers via User Management → Client Assignments, ensuring each client has a designated relationship officer.

Creating a Loan Application

Loan Parameters

  • • Loan Type: Personal, Business, Agricultural, etc.
  • • Principal: Amount in GH₵
  • • Interest Rate: Annual percentage
  • • Term: Duration in months
  • • Interest Method: Flat or Reducing Balance

Repayment Configuration

  • • Frequency: Daily, Weekly, Fortnightly, Monthly
  • • Penalty Type: None, Flat, % Overdue, Daily Rate
  • • Grace Days: Days before penalty applies
  • • Purpose: Reason for the loan
  • • Notes: Additional information
🔒 Automatic Compliance Checks

Before a loan can be approved, the system automatically checks: (1) Single obligor limit based on your BoG tier, (2) Maximum loan amount per borrower, (3) Client affordability based on income vs expenses, (4) Existing exposure to the same borrower.

Posting Repayments

Navigate to Repayments from the sidebar. Search for the loan by client name or loan ID. Enter the payment amount, method (Cash, Mobile Money, Bank Transfer), and reference number. The system automatically splits the payment into principal, interest, and penalty portions based on the loan's amortisation schedule. Outstanding balances update in real time.

Every field collection captures GPS coordinates, timestamp, and officer ID. Managers can verify that collections actually occurred at the client's location. If a Field Officer submits a collection from a different location, it's flagged for review.

Regulatory Compliance

For Regulators: Security & Compliance Verification

This section is specifically for BoG examiners and compliance officers evaluating MFI Clarity for regulatory approval.

✅ Data Isolation

Row-Level Security (RLS) at PostgreSQL level ensures complete multi-tenant data isolation. No application-level filtering — enforced at the database engine.

✅ Audit Trail

Immutable activity_audit_log table records every action with user_id, timestamp, IP address, entity_type, old_values, and new_values. Cannot be modified or deleted.

✅ Access Control

Five RBAC roles enforced at API level via database security functions (has_role, is_executive, is_board_member). Policies check user_id + org_id on every query.

✅ Encryption

AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit. All secrets managed via secure vault. No plaintext credentials in application code.

Regulatory Reports

Automated BoG Compliance Reporting

The Regulatory Reports module is the heart of MFI Clarity's compliance engine. Here's how to use each tool.

🏦 Capital Adequacy Ratio (CAR) Calculator

The CAR calculator automatically computes your institution's capital adequacy ratio based on your risk-weighted assets and net worth. The threshold is set based on your BoG tier (typically 10%).

Step 1
Enter net worth in Settings
Step 2
System calculates risk-weighted assets
Step 3
CAR ratio displayed with compliance status

💧 Liquidity Ratio Calculator

Monitors your institution's liquid assets against short-term liabilities. Alerts you when you approach the minimum threshold, giving you time to take corrective action before BoG examination.

📋 Prudential Returns Tracker

Tracks your reporting deadlines based on your tier's frequency (daily, weekly, monthly, or quarterly). Shows what's due, what's been submitted, and what's overdue. Never miss a deadline again.

🔍 AML Reports

Anti-Money Laundering reports flag suspicious transactions, high-risk clients, and large cash movements. Supports Ghana's AML/CFT framework requirements.

📊 BoG Bucket Classification

Every loan is automatically classified into BoG buckets based on days overdue: Current (0 days), OLEM (1-30 days), Substandard (31-90 days), Doubtful (91-180 days), Loss (180+ days). Provision amounts are calculated using the standard rates.

Troubleshooting

Common Questions & Solutions

Quick answers to the most frequently asked questions from MFI Clarity users.

❓ I can't see any data after logging in

Solution: Your account needs to be linked to an organisation. Contact your Admin to add you via User Management. If you're the first user, complete the Organisation Onboarding to create your institution.

❓ I can't approve a loan

Solution: Only Admin and Manager roles can approve loans. If you're a Teller or Field Officer, the loan you created will appear in the manager's workflow for review and approval.

❓ My field collections aren't syncing

Solution: Check the network indicator in the top bar. If it shows "Offline", your data is stored locally and will sync when connectivity returns. If you're online but sync isn't working, go to Sync Conflicts to check for and resolve any data conflicts.

❓ The CAR calculator shows "N/A"

Solution: You need to enter your institution's net worth in Settings → Organisation → BoG Tier Settings. The CAR cannot be calculated without the net worth (equity) figure.

❓ How do I export a report for BoG?

Solution: Navigate to the relevant report (e.g., Portfolio Aging, Regulatory Reports). Click the "Export CSV" or "Export PDF" button at the top of any table. The exported file includes all data with proper formatting for regulatory submission.

❓ Can multiple people use the system at the same time?

Solution: Yes! MFI Clarity is a cloud-based system. Unlimited users can work simultaneously. Each person logs in with their own account, and all changes are reflected in real time for all users.

❓ What happens if I accidentally post the wrong repayment?

Solution: Contact your Admin. All transactions are logged in the audit trail with the original values. The Admin can review the incorrect entry and the Manager can process a corrective transaction.
